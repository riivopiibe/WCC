Night and day blinds

Can be ordered as normal roller blinds, mini roller blinds and cassette roller blinds.Night and day mini blinds can be attached to the window frame with screws.Fastening mechanisms can be selected in different colors: white, brown, walnut, golden oak and mahogany.Mini blinds are transparent Tamil side ropes that are attached to the window frame.This ensures that the blind is fixed and correctly positioned when in the ventilation position.Night and day cassette blind has a fabric in the cassette and slats on the side.The cassette is attached to the window frame, so that the window can be conveniently opened without the blind falling in front of the window.We use aluminum cassettes, which are strong and durable. Cassettes can be chosen from different colors: white, brown, gray and various imitations of wood.The price of the product depends on the substance price group. The price increases depending on the group number.
