Pleated blinds

This modern solution of the inner sun shade is characterized by high functionality and the possibility of using it in unusual window shapes such as a triangle, trapezoid and even an arch. Pleated blinds are also suitable for roof windows. Thanks to the cable guidance, it can be performed in any plane. A wide range of materials will allow you to decorate the most sophisticated interior. Trendiness, universality and practicality are combined in the folding blinds. It is a textile window covering that can be closed and opened in folds, which is both practical and a stylish solution for any room. Light and textured pleated blinds decorates and brings coziness to the room.
Pleated blinds are sturdy and durable, which makes them a long-term solution for sun protection, room temperature regulation and privacy. Since pleated blinds is made of moisture, mildew and bleach resistant materials, it is easy to clean and can be used for a long time. You also do not have to worry about the pleated blinds wrinkles: The blind is impregnated with a special agent that ensures the folds of the curtain for many years.

'A pleated blind is an excellent solution for windows with special shapes. Since pleated blinds can be made in various shapes, they are also common for covering round, oval and other non-standard window shapes. It is possible to install the blinds both on the top of the window frame and inside, which in turn provides a scope for different window shapes and thicknesses.

The special feature of the folding blind is its practicality, because the blind can be opened both from above and below, which gives the opportunity to adjust the blind as needed. For example, while the sunlight falls into the room, the hidden bottom protects from the eyes of passers-by.

Which room is a pleated BLINDS suitable for?

Folding blinds are suitable for any window, with transparent, semi-blackout and blackout material, but they are an excellent solution especially in the living room and bedroom. In the living room, we recommend a pleated semi-blackout blind that blocks excess sunlight but still allows daylight to pass through. However, if you want to watch movies or play games in the dark, blackout pleated blinds are also suitable for the living room.

Also in the bedroom, blackout pleated blinds allow you to make the room as dark as possible to avoid the sun in the early morning hours and disturbed sleep. It is also possible to place the pleated blinds at will, which provides enormous opportunities to regulate the light.

-you are looking for a practical solution for the office, to cover wall, roof or ceiling windows.
-You want to cover a window with a special shape.-
You want to cover the terrace or balcony window.
-There is little space to install the window cover.
-If necessary, you want to expose the entire window glass and need a compact solution.
-You need an excellent solution for damp rooms.
-You want a window cover with an effective design and color selection.
-You want to open the curtain both from below and above.
