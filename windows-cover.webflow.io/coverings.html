Blinds

Textile blinds are the most commonly used window coverings,which are practical and provide good protection from sunlight. Blinds can be attached to the window frame, window opening or wall. It is possible to choose between fabrics with a wide variety of colors and patterns. We offer transparent, semi-darkening and blackout fabrics in our assortment. Our assortment also includes mini blinds, which can be attached only to the window frame and have a maximum width of 140 cm and height of 180 cm. The blind is fixed to the window frame with screws. Mini blinds are transparent Tamil side ropes that are attached to the window frame. This fixes the blind and keeps it in position in the ventilation position. The maximum width of larger blinds can be selected up to 3.4 m, and for all blinds we use the mechanisms required depending on the size. For the lighter weight of the blind, we use aluminum coated bottom edge. In the case of semi-blackout fabrics, the bottom weight can also be sewn into the fabric. The price of the product depends on the price group of the fabric. The price increases with the number of price groups from smaller to larger. Fabric options by price group.
Group I - A,
group II - F,
group III - B,
group IV - C,
group V - fireproof fabrics..

Night and day blinds

Day and night blinds can be ordered as normal roller blinds (maximum width 220 cm), mini blinds (maximum width 140 cm) and cassette blinds (maximum width 160 cm). Night and day mini blinds can be fixed to the window frame with screws. the fastening mechanisms can be selected in different colors: white, brown, walnut, golden oak and mahogany. Mini blinds are transparent Tamil side ropes that are attached to the window frame. This ensures that the blind is fixed and remains properly in place in the ventilation position. The day and night cassette blind is located inside the fabric cassette and has slats on the sides. The cassette is fixed to the window frame, so that the window can be opened comfortably without the blind falling in front of the window. We use aluminium cassettes that are strong and durable. You can choose between different colours for the cassettes: white, brown, grey and different imitations of wood. The price of the product depends on the fabric price group. The price increases with the number of groups. Fabric options by price group. I price group - DN, II price group - DNA, III group - DNB, IV group - DNC

Pleated blinds

Pleated curtains are effective and practical and help to protect the room from sunlight. Pleated curtains are made of fabric and can be opened and closed in different ways: both from top to bottom and from bottom to top. Folding curtains are a good solution for specially shaped windows and skylights. Folding curtains can be fitted in two different ways: 1)in front of the window glass in the depth of the frame - the folding curtain runs along the window glass.We can offer 2 different profiles.If the depth of the window frame is 1.3 cm or more, we canoffer you a folding curtain with a maximum width of 1000 mm and a height of 2000 mm. If the depth of the window frame is 1.5 cm, we can offer a folding curtain with a maximum width of 1200 mm and a height of 1500 mm. 2) on top of the window frame - offers the possibility to cover the window to the desired extent. It is also suitable for windows where the depth of the opening is too small, less than 1.3 cm. There are many fabrics available with different properties (transparent, semi-darkening, blackout) in different colours and patterns. Honeycomb fabric is also available. Honeycomb fabric is a double fabric with a honeycomb cross-section where the curtain cords run between two layers of fabric. We also make pleated curtains with a maximum width of up to 220 cm in one piece, controlled by the cords on the sides. The price of the product depends on the fabric price group. The price increases depending on the group number. Fabric options by price group. Group I, Group II, Group III, IV group, V group.

INSECT SCREENS

A mosquito net is a great way to protect your home or office from mosquitoes and other nasty insects. The frame of the mosquito net is made of aluminum. The mosquito net frame is available in white, brown or anthracite gray. We offer different solutions for windows: Clip-on mosquito net or cassette mosquito net. The cassette mosquito net is also suitable for skylights, where it is placed in front of the window opening. It is possible to install hinged doors or inserts in the doors. The net is available in two different colors: black or gray.

Skylight blinds

Suitable for the following skylights: Velux, Fakro, Okpol. Skylight blinds are installed directly on the window frame. Thanks to the spring mechanism it is possible to leave the fabric blind in any position. For windows that are too high, we offer a bar for moving the skylight, which can be ordered in three sizes: 1 m, 1.5 m and 2 m. The following fabrics are available: translucent, semi-obscuring, blackout and sun-reflecting.

PANEL BLINDS

Panel curtains are ideal for large and wide windows. Panel curtains can be installed on the wall and on the ceiling. Different solutions can be chosen for opening the panels. The panels can be opened in one direction or in two different directions. Depending on the width of the fabric, you can choose between a different number of panels: 2-8 panels. The panels can be opened with a rope, a control lever or even with a motorized solution. Fabric selection by price class groups.

textile curtains

Textile curtains are the best way to create a cozy interior. We offer various solutions for curtains. The selection includes blackout and semi-darkening side curtain fabrics as well as various tulle curtain fabrics with a wide range of colors. We also offer Roman blinds made of various textiles. As a solution for curtain support systems, we offer various curtain tracks. The curtains can be mounted both on the ceiling and on the wall. To get a price quote, write us how long you want curtains on a rail and also the height of the room. We offer a separate measuring service, where the measurer has all the fabric selection with him.
